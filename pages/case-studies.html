Case Study: San Antonio Restaurant Group

Industry: Restaurant / Hospitality

Location: San Antonio, TX (3 locations along the River Walk and Stone Oak area)

The Challenge

A multi-location restaurant group in San Antonio was struggling with low online visibility. Despite serving award-winning cuisine, their website barely appeared in local search results. Online reservations were declining, and competitors with stronger digital presences were capturing the majority of search traffic for dining-related keywords in San Antonio.

Our Strategy

SEO Agency LLC developed a comprehensive local SEO strategy that included optimizing their Google Business Profiles for all three San Antonio locations, creating location-specific landing pages, building a content marketing plan around San Antonio food culture and events, implementing structured data markup for restaurants, and earning high-quality local backlinks from San Antonio media outlets and food bloggers.

The Results

  • 312% increase in organic search traffic within 8 months
  • Top 3 rankings for 15 high-value San Antonio dining keywords
  • 89% increase in online reservation requests
  • 4x growth in Google Maps visibility across all locations

Case Study: San Antonio Home Services Company

Industry: Home Services / HVAC

Location: Greater San Antonio metro area, including Boerne and New Braunfels

The Challenge

An HVAC company serving the greater San Antonio area was heavily reliant on paid advertising for lead generation. Their organic presence was minimal, and cost-per-lead through PPC was increasing each quarter. They needed a sustainable organic channel to reduce their dependence on paid media and lower their overall customer acquisition cost.

Our Strategy

SEO Agency LLC built a multi-pronged SEO strategy featuring service-area pages targeting every neighborhood and suburb across the San Antonio metro region, seasonal content campaigns aligned with Texas weather patterns, a technical SEO foundation with schema markup for local services, aggressive Google Business Profile optimization, and a link-building campaign focused on San Antonio home improvement publications and community organizations.

The Results

  • 198% increase in organic leads within 10 months
  • Top 5 rankings for 30+ HVAC service keywords across San Antonio
  • 42% reduction in overall cost-per-lead by shifting budget from PPC to organic
  • Coverage expanded to rank in 12 surrounding San Antonio area cities

Case Study: San Antonio E-Commerce Brand

Industry: E-Commerce / Retail

Location: San Antonio, TX (online store with local warehouse)

The Challenge

A San Antonio-based e-commerce brand selling handcrafted home goods had strong local word-of-mouth but almost zero organic search visibility. Their product pages lacked optimization, their site architecture made crawling difficult, and they had no content strategy to capture informational search traffic related to their product categories.

Our Strategy

The Results

  • First-page rankings for 40+ product-category keywords

  • 35% of total revenue now attributed to organic search, up from 8%
