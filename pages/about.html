Our Story

Built on Results, Not Promises

SEO Agency LLC was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: help San Antonio businesses grow through honest, effective search engine optimization. We saw too many agencies making big promises with black-hat tactics that ultimately hurt their clients.

We took a different approach. Every strategy we build is grounded in data, aligned with Google's guidelines, and focused on sustainable long-term growth. Today, we're proud to be San Antonio's highest-rated SEO company with a 98% client retention rate.

Our team of 25+ SEO professionals brings together expertise in technical SEO, content strategy, link building, and local search optimization. We've completed over 500 successful campaigns across every major industry — from healthcare and legal to e-commerce and SaaS.
