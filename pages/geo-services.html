FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About GEO

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?

GEO is the practice of optimizing your brand and content so AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, and Perplexity recommend your business in their generated answers. It focuses on entity building, structured data, and brand authority signals that AI models rely on.

How is GEO different from SEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for ranked links on search engine result pages. GEO optimizes for AI-generated answers and recommendations. SEO targets keywords and backlinks, while GEO targets entity recognition, brand mentions, and the trusted data sources that large language models rely on to generate responses.

Can AI search really drive customers to my business?

Yes. Millions of consumers now use AI tools to find local businesses and make purchase decisions. When someone asks ChatGPT for the best dentist in Alamo Heights or the top-rated roofer in Stone Oak, being the recommended answer drives high-intent traffic directly to your business.

How do you get a business recommended by ChatGPT?

We build your brand's digital footprint through entity injection on trusted platforms, authoritative mentions on high-quality sources, comprehensive structured data, and content optimization that helps AI models understand your business, services, and why you are a top recommendation in San Antonio.

How long does GEO take to work?

Most clients begin seeing improved AI visibility within 60 to 90 days as AI models update their knowledge bases. Full results typically materialize within 4 to 6 months. GEO is a long-term strategy — consistent brand-building efforts compound over time and create a durable competitive advantage.
