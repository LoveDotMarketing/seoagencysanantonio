Our Process

How We Deliver Content Marketing Results

A five-step process designed to turn content into a reliable growth engine for your San Antonio business.

Step 1: Content Audit — We analyze your existing content to identify what is performing, what is underperforming, and what is missing. This audit reveals quick wins such as content that can be refreshed for better rankings and gaps where competitors are capturing traffic you should own.
Step 2: Strategy & Editorial Calendar — Based on audit findings and keyword research, we build a monthly editorial calendar. Every topic is mapped to a target keyword, a stage of the buyer journey, and a business objective. You approve the calendar before any writing begins.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Content Marketing

How often should I publish new content?

For most San Antonio businesses, publishing 2-4 high-quality blog posts per month delivers the best balance of consistency and depth. Quality always beats quantity — one well-researched, optimized article outperforms five thin posts. We build an editorial calendar tailored to your capacity and goals.

What types of content work best for SEO?

Long-form blog posts, service pages, how-to guides, and locally focused content tend to perform best for SEO. The key is matching content format to search intent. Informational queries need comprehensive guides, while commercial queries need service and comparison pages. We analyze intent for every keyword we target.

How do you measure content marketing success?

We track organic traffic growth, keyword rankings, time on page, bounce rate, conversion rate, leads generated, and revenue attributed to content. Monthly reports show exactly which pieces are driving results and where we should double down for your San Antonio business.

Do you write content for specific industries?

Yes. We create content for healthcare, legal, home services, restaurants, real estate, and many other industries across San Antonio. Our writers research your industry thoroughly and can work with your subject matter experts to ensure accuracy and authority in every piece.

How long before content marketing shows results?

Content marketing is a long-term strategy. Most businesses see initial traffic gains within 2-3 months, with significant momentum building by months 4-6. Compounding returns mean your best results come after 6-12 months of consistent publishing and optimization.
