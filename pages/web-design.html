FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Web Design

How much does a website cost in San Antonio?

Website costs vary based on scope and complexity. A professional small business website typically starts around $3,000 to $5,000. E-commerce sites and custom web applications range from $7,500 to $25,000 or more depending on features. Contact us for a free custom quote tailored to your needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most business websites take 4 to 8 weeks from kickoff to launch. E-commerce sites and larger projects may take 8 to 12 weeks. Timelines depend on content readiness, design complexity, number of revisions, and how quickly feedback is provided during the review stages.

Do you build WordPress sites?

Yes. We build on WordPress, Shopify, and custom platforms depending on your needs. WordPress is our most popular option for business websites because it offers flexibility, strong SEO capabilities, and easy content management for non-technical users.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Every website we build is mobile-responsive by default. We design mobile-first, ensuring your site looks and performs beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Google uses mobile-first indexing, so a mobile-friendly site is essential for SEO.

Do you offer ongoing website maintenance?

Yes. We offer monthly maintenance plans that include security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, backups, and technical support. Ongoing maintenance keeps your website fast, secure, and optimized as your business grows.
